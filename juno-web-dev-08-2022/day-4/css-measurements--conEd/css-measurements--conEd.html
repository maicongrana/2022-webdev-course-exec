Rulers Rule

Measuring things since the dawn of time

You know how much we love rulers here.

Types

Other names for rulers include: Measuring device, protractor, yardstick, tape measure, desk rule, steel rule.

Song

Draw a long straight line, Or measure the length of a vine, All you need to do is align, The ruler is a tool so fine!

The Past

A Lick, is a measurement from the tip of thumb to the tip of index finger used by the Greeks, and a Pace is around the length of a double step used by the Romans.
